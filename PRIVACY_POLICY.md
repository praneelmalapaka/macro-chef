# MacroChef Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

MacroChef is a social calorie and macro tracking app. This policy is a starter policy for internal or closed testing and should be reviewed before a public launch.

## Data We Collect

- Email address for account creation, login, and email verification.
- Username and display name for profile and social features.
- Optional profile information such as bio, avatar URL, calorie goal, and macro goals.
- Food logs and calorie/macro data that you enter.
- Friend requests and friend relationships.
- Basic technical data needed to operate the service, such as authentication tokens and API request metadata.

## Why We Collect Data

- To create and secure your account.
- To verify your email address.
- To show your calorie and macro dashboard.
- To save food logs and historical tracking data.
- To power social features such as profile search, friend requests, and friend-visible food logs.
- To respect profile privacy settings.

## Data Sharing

MacroChef does not sell personal data. Profile and food log visibility depends on your privacy settings:

- Public profiles can be viewed by logged-in verified users.
- Private profiles and food logs are visible only to you and accepted friends.

## Data Deletion

Users can request account and data deletion by contacting support. Account deletion should remove profile data, food logs, friend requests, and friendships associated with the account.

## Security

Passwords are hashed on the backend. Email verification and authentication secrets are stored only on backend infrastructure. The Android app does not contain SMTP credentials, database credentials, JWT secrets, or signing passwords.

## Contact

For support or deletion requests, contact: support@example.com
